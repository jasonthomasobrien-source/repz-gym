Where should the weightlifting video go?

And how should we alternate section backgrounds?

Key consideration:

The video is dynamic and powerful. We need to decide: should it be a static background layer (parallax effect) or a full hero replacement? And how do alternating dark/light sections interact with it?

Alternating Section Backgrounds

All three options work better with alternating dark/light sections. Current site uses all dark (`--bg`). We could alternate:

  • Dark sections: `--bg` (#0a0a0a) — Hero, Classes, Team, etc.
  • Light sections: `--surface` (#141414 slightly lighter, or a true light gray for admin-style cards)
  • Effect: Visual separation + makes alternating content layers stand out

This naturally supports parallax video in the middle — video can sit in a light-background section while cards float over it.
