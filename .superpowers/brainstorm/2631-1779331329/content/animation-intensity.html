How energetic should the scroll animations be?

The design system already has some motion (section reveals, card hovers). We're adding more. What's the right energy level?

Subtle & Refined

Simple opacity fades + gentle parallax. Sections fade in as they scroll into view. Images behind text move slowly (30% of scroll speed). No scale, no slide. Content feels steady and professional.

  • Parallax on hero video only
  • Fade-in (opacity) on section headlines
  • Fade-in on class/trainer cards (staggered)
  • No transforms or scale effects

Vibe: Confident, controlled. Feels premium. Content doesn't distract from itself.

← Slides in from right

Energetic & Bold

Slide-ins + parallax + staggered reveals. Content slides in from left/right as sections come into view. Images move faster parallax. Card items stagger in. Feels dynamic and energetic, matches "TRAIN HARD" vibe.

  • Video parallax at 50% scroll speed (noticeable depth)
  • Headlines slide in from left/right
  • Class cards slide up + fade in (staggered 80ms apart)
  • Trainer cards scale up slightly (1.02x) as they fade in

Vibe: High energy, gym-poster feel. Draws eye through page. More "show-y" but still on-brand.

Hybrid (Recommended)

Bold animations on key moments, subtle elsewhere. Hero and registration CTA get slide/parallax treatment. Class/trainer cards fade in. Section headlines fade. Balances energy with sophistication.

  • Hero video parallax (40% scroll speed)
  • Registration CTA slides up on scroll
  • Section headlines fade in
  • Class/trainer cards fade + stagger (no scale)

Vibe: Best of both worlds. Dramatic moments draw attention, but content stays readable. Professional with personality.

Design system baseline (already in place):

  • Buttons: 120ms hover darkening (no scale)
  • Card hovers: 200ms image scale 1.04, overlay deepens
  • Section reveals (already in spec): opacity + 16px Y-translate, 400ms ease-out
  • All animations respect prefers-reduced-motion

We're building on top of these existing animations.
